# Frameworks Index — Quick Reference

One-line descriptions. Use the framework selection guide in SKILL.md to pick the right one.

## Strategy
- **Five Forces** (Porter) — industry attractiveness via 5 competitive pressures
- **Value Chain** (Porter) — primary + support activities, find cost/differentiation advantage
- **7S** — 7 interconnected org elements: strategy/structure/systems/shared values/skills/staff/style
- **VRIO** — resource-based: is it Valuable, Rare, Inimitable, Organization-supported?
- **3 Horizons** — H1 optimize core, H2 scale emerging, H3 explore future
- **Ansoff Matrix** — growth via market penetration/development, product development, diversification
- **Growth-Share Matrix** — Stars/Cash Cows/Dogs/Question Marks by growth rate vs market share
- **Nine-Box Matrix** — portfolio analysis: industry attractiveness vs business strength
- **Blue Ocean** (Kim & Mauborgne) — create uncontested market space; Strategy Canvas + Four Actions (eliminate/reduce/raise/create)
- **Wardley Mapping** — value chain evolution from genesis to commodity; informs build/buy/outsource
- **Scenario Planning** — multiple plausible futures to stress-test strategies

## Problem Solving
- **MECE** (Minto) — mutually exclusive, collectively exhaustive decomposition
- **Issue Tree** — problem → MECE sub-problems → data needs at leaves
- **Hypothesis Tree** — problem → MECE hypotheses → kill tests
- **7-Step Problem Solving** — define, disaggregate, prioritize, plan, analyze, synthesize, recommend
- **Pyramid Principle** (Minto) — answer first, then supporting arguments, then evidence
- **SCQA** — Situation, Complication, Question, Answer (narrative framing)

## Decision Making
- **RAPID** — Recommend, Agree, Perform, Input, Decide
- **Decision Matrix** — weighted criteria evaluation of options
- **Pre-Mortem** — assume failure, work backward to identify causes
- **Second-Order Thinking** — consequences of consequences
- **Inversion** — instead of "how to succeed," ask "how could this fail?"

## Financial / Sizing
- **Profit Tree** — Revenue (Price x Volume) minus Cost (Fixed + Variable)
- **Revenue Tree** — segments x products x channels x pricing
- **TAM/SAM/SOM** — total/serviceable/obtainable market sizing
- **Unit Economics** — LTV, CAC, payback period, contribution margin
- **Waterfall / Bridge** — incremental contributions to a total

## Operations / Organization
- **ADKAR** (Prosci) — Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement
- **Kotter's 8-Step** — urgency → coalition → vision → communicate → empower → wins → build → anchor
- **Lewin's Change Model** — unfreeze → change → refreeze
- **Influence Model** — role modeling, understanding, confidence, reinforcement
- **Customer Journey Map** — touchpoints, emotions, pain points across the experience
- **NPS / Loyalty** — promoters, passives, detractors → growth correlation

## Innovation
- **Jobs to Be Done** (Christensen) — what "job" does the customer hire this product for?
- **Business Model Canvas** (Osterwalder) — 9 building blocks of a business model
- **Value Proposition Canvas** — customer jobs/pains/gains vs product features/pain relievers/gain creators
- **Advantage Matrix** — number of ways to compete vs size of advantage → fragmented/specialized/stalemate/volume

## Pricing
- **Value-Based** — price to perceived customer value
- **Cost-Plus** — cost + margin
- **Competitive** — price relative to competitors
- **Dynamic** — real-time adjustment by demand/supply
- **Freemium** — free base + paid premium
